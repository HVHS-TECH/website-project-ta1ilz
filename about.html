About Hypercars

What Are Hypercars?

Hypercars represent the peak of automotive performance. These vehicles go beyond traditional supercars by pushing limits in speed, aerodynamics, and innovation. Many hypercars feature hybrid systems, lightweight carbon fiber bodies, and engines capable of producing over 1,000 horsepower.

Examples include the Bugatti Chiron, Koenigsegg Jesko, and McLaren P1 — machines built not just for driving, but for redefining what cars can achieve.
